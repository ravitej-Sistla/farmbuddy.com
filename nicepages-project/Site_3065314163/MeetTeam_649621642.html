About Us

WHO ARE WE?

​​We, Abhay Mane, Sanket Vathare and Shrivardhan Joshi, the founders of FARMBUDDY NATURALS brand, a while ago, were unhappy with the fact that most of the commercial honey available in the market was extremely processed and pasteurized and never felt genuine and natural. That’s where our Journey began and we decided to get away from our Engineering backgrounds and plunge into this beautiful and sweet world of Honey Bees and decided to bring the most natural form of Honey, RAW, and UNPROCESSED HONEY under our Brand “FARMBUDDY NATURALS”, directly from the Hives to The Table.

​MEET OUR EXPERT TEAM

Sanket Vathare

​A Nature enthusiast, a master in Materials and metal testing and an extremely focused individual, Sanket is a Mechanical Engineer with a Masters in Metallurgy turned into a world class Beekeeper and looks after the Procurement, Quality and Production side of the business. An expert in Beekeeping, Sanket is a key person in making FARMBUDDY NATURALS brand one of the most sorted after and unique Raw Honey brands available in the market.

​​Shrivardhan Joshi

​Based out of California USA, an extremely talented singer and musician and an avid nature lover, Shrivardhan is responsible for the Sales and distribution side of the business. Shri has a Masters degree in Industrial Engineering from SUNY Buffalo and has a vast experience in the Health and Safety sector. Shri is the reason behind FARMBUDDY NATURALS success in the US market

Abhay Mane

​Extremely passionate about his health, an avid runner, and a hobbyist photographer, Abhay looks after the Marketing and logistical side of the business. Abhay has a Masters Degree in Industrial Engineering from Oklahoma State University, Stillwater and 15 years’ of experience in the Steel Foundry business.
