We are Bee Keepers, Yes We are, and we take the Pride in Keeping our Honey Bees absolutely safe and away from any type of disease and feeding them with their own food, HONEY, r​​ather than sugar syrup which is a practice followed by most.

We are proud of presenting absolutely Raw and Unprocessed Honey, to our valuable Customers, from variety of flora available from all parts of India, right from Maharashtra, where we collect our most valuable Raw Forest Honey to the Himalayas where Raw Eucalyptus Honey is collected to Rajasthan for Raw Ajwain and many more.

Raw Eucalyptus Honey

Collected from the flowers of eucalyptus globulus trees found at the foothills of the Great Himalayas, our Raw Eucalyptus Honey is light in color and less viscous as compared to our other varieties. An extremely refreshing and pleasant aroma will keep you craving for more.

Raw Sunflower Honey

Our Raw Sunflower Honey comes from the finest Sunflower plantation in the western part of India. Our Sunflower Honey is light in color and
smooth in texture with a very soothing aroma of the flower itself. We make sure our Honey has more than 45% sunflower pollen as we do not process any of our Honey

Raw Tulsi (Wild Basil) Honey

Our Raw Tulsi Honey comes from the wild Tulsi (Holy Basil) flora found in the northern part of India. Amber-colored, thick, and creamy with warm herbal undertones, Our Raw Tulsi Honey has a strong, bold, and distinctive flavor.

Raw Forest Honey

Collected from the famous Forests of Western India, our Raw Forest Honey is smooth, thick, and full of Fun and Nutrition. Collected from the wild local flora available in the Dajipur forests, our Raw Forest Honey is one of its kind and is exclusive and rare.
